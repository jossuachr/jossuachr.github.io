Winner of Best in Show for our end-to-end data analysis and modeling project.

Project Overview

As part of the RStudio Wizards team from Truman State University, we tackled a real-world dataset provided by industry partners. Our deliverables included:

  • Exploratory Data Analysis to uncover key patterns.
  • Statistical modeling and machine learning to predict target outcomes.
  • Interactive Shiny dashboard for stakeholder exploration.
  • Data visualizations in Tableau and ggplot2.
  • A comprehensive report and presentation summarizing insights.
