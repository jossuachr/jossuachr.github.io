DataFest 2025 – Best in Show

As lead analyst of the RStudio Wizards team, I designed and implemented an end‑to‑end data science workflow on commercial lease data, combining statistical modeling and geospatial visualization to deliver actionable insights and an interactive dashboard—earning Best in Show.

Business Problem

Our sponsors sought a transparent method to:

  • Quantify the impact of economic indicators (e.g., unemployment) on lease pricing.
  • Detect leases that were under‑ or over‑valued relative to model predictions.
  • Provide an interactive map for stakeholders to explore market dynamics.

We aimed to bridge data engineering, predictive modeling, and user‑friendly geovisualization into a single, reproducible pipeline.

Data Loading & Cleaning

We loaded four CSVs—Leases, Unemployment, Price & Availability, Major Market—and merged them by year, quarter, state, and market. Key steps:

  • Grouped by lease_id to compute overall_rent (sum of payments).
  • Imputed numeric fields with median and categorical fields with mode functions.
  • Engineered price_category indicating under‑ or over‑valuation based on model residuals.

Random Forest Modeling

We trained a Random Forest regressor to predict overall_rent:

  • Data split: 80/20 training/testing stratified by year to preserve temporal structure.
  • Tuning: mtry (number of variables tried at each split) and min_n via 5‑fold cross‑validation.
  • Final metrics on test set: R² = 0.85, RMSE ≈ \$45.

Interactive Geospatial Map

We integrated a Leaflet map to visualize average model residuals (under/over valuation) by market. To embed this map on your page:

  1. Include Leaflet CSS in your <head>:
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://unpkg.com/leaflet/dist/leaflet.css" />
  2. Include Leaflet JS before the closing </body>:
    <script src="https://unpkg.com/leaflet/dist/leaflet.js"></script>
  3. Add a container div:
    <div id="map" class="w-full h-96 rounded shadow"></div>
  4. Initialize the map with your GeoJSON market data:
    const map = L.map('map').setView([39, -98], 4);
L.tileLayer('https://{s}.tile.openstreetmap.org/{z}/{x}/{y}.png').addTo(map);
L.geoJson(marketGeoJson, { style: feature => ({ fillColor: colorScale(feature.properties.residual), weight: 1 }) }).addTo(map);

Key Insights & Recommendations

Our integrated analysis provided:

  • A reliable predictive model for lease pricing, capturing 85% of variance.
  • Interpretability through ALE, revealing economic thresholds and saturation effects.
  • A geospatial interface for pinpointing under‑ or over‑valued markets in real time.

Next steps: deploy the model via a Plumber API for live scoring, and enhance the dashboard with user-driven scenario simulations.
